Seed Calculator: Privacy Policy

Beijing SeedV Technology Co., Ltd. built the Seed Calculator (aka. SeedCalc) app as an Open Source app. SeedCalc is provided by Beijing SeedV Technology Co., Ltd. at no cost and is intended for use as is.

Information Collection and Use

SeedCalc does not collect, transmit, distribute or sell your data.

SeedCalc and its developer take your privacy very seriously. Beyond the information Apple provides to developers that you can decide to provide, SeedCalc uses no third-party analytics or advertising frameworks. SeedCalc logs no information on you and has no interest in doing such.

Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update our Privacy Policy from time to time. Thus, you are advised to review this page periodically for any changes. We will notify you of any changes by posting the new Privacy Policy on this page.
